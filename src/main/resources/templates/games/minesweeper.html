MINESWEEPER (DO MIN)

Game offline giai do co dien. Mo cac o an toan, cam co o nghi co min, va hoan thanh ban co ma khong kich no.

Offline san sang

Choi ngay

Ho tro 3 muc do co ban (de/trung binh/kho), mo o lan dau an toan, cam co va tu dong mo vung rong khi gap o trong.

Bat dau - De (9x9 / 10 min) Trung binh (16x16 / 40 min) Kho (16x30 / 99 min)

Cach choi nhanh

  • Click trai de mo o.
  • Click phai de cam co (desktop).
  • Mobile/tablet dung nut "Che do dat co" de chuyen thao tac.
  • Thang khi mo het o khong co min.
Ve trang chu Thu vien game

Ghi chu

  • Minesweeper hien tai la game offline client-side, khong can dang nhap.
  • Co the doi muc do ngay trong trang choi ma khong can quay lai.
  • Da duoc dua vao Game Hub de truy cap thong nhat tu Trang chu va Thu vien game.
