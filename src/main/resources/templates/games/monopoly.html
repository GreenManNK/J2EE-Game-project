Kinh te co can doi

Su kien lam xoay van

Luat nhanh tren ban nay

Rules
  • Moi luot tung 2 xuc xac, di qua GO nhan 200$ va kich hoat o dang dung.
  • Dat trong co the mua ngay; dat cua doi thu tinh tien thue theo set so huu, nha va hotel.
  • So huu tron nhom mau mo khoa xay nha, sau do nang len hotel theo quy tac can bang cung nhom.
  • Neu can tien de tra no, nguoi choi co the ban nha theo nua gia va the chap tai san truoc khi tuyen bo pha san.
  • Van ket thuc khi chi con 1 nguoi chua pha san hoac khi bam chot van de xep hang theo gia tri rong.

Nhung gi da co trong engine

Feature
1.Setup 2-4 nguoi, tuy chon von khoi diem va vao van moi ngay tren trang.
2.Board render day du, token tung nguoi, free parking pot va selected tile detail.
3.Chance / Community Chest, Jail, utility, railroad, mortgage, buy/sell nha va hotel.
4.Bang xep hang net worth + log van dau cap nhat realtime khi choi local.
