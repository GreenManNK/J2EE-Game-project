Nhip gameplay TLMN + Blackjack

Gameplay
  • TLMN online vao tran theo room 4 nguoi, chia bai va chay vong theo luot.
  • Bo bai hop le duoc kiem tra theo luat; he thong tu dong reset vong khi can.
  • Cuoi van cap nhat diem, phat va delta tung nguoi de vao van moi ngay.
  • Blackjack trong cards hub co nhip rieng: dat cuoc -> hit/stand/double -> chia tien.

Thanh tuu danh bai

Achievement
  • Mo man TLMN: choi du tran online dau tien.
  • Day dai phong choi: ve nhat 3 van lien tiep.
  • Bung no diem so: dat moc +100 diem tong trong ngay.
  • Blackjack master: dat blackjack tu nhien 3 van.
